Philosophy

The Colegio De La Santisima Eucarista Inc. as a Catholic Mission School, believes that true Christian education nurtures the whole person in faith, knowledge, and character. It provides a safe and caring community where every learner's God given potential is developed through and . Rooted in the Eucharist, the school is committed to forming life-long learners who pursue excellence. live with integrity, and serve others as compassionate and globally responsible citizens.


Vision

The as a Catholic Mission School, envisions itself as a learning community that provides affordable and excellent education in a safe and nurturing environment, where students are inspired to achieve their full potential, develop essential skills, and become globally competent and life-long learners guided by the values of SERVICE, WISDOM, AND TRUTH


Mission

To achieve the above vision, we commit ourselves to:

  1. Provide affordable and quality Catholic education that promotes academic excellence and moral integrity.
  2. Build a God-centered community that cultivates faith, compassion, and a spirit of service.
  3. Develop the learners' skills, talents, and values to help students achieve their full potential and prepare them for a global society.
  4. Maintain a safe, inclusive, and nurturing learning enviromment that supports holistic growth.
  5. Filipino catholic community with Eucharistic devotion that is committed to promote gender equality, patriotism, justice and peace.
